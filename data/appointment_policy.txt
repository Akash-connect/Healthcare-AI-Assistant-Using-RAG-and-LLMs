Appointment Scheduling Policy

Patients can book appointments through the patient portal, phone call, front desk, or approved telehealth system.

Available departments include cardiology, dermatology, general medicine, pediatrics, orthopedics, and neurology.

Appointments are subject to doctor availability and department schedule.

Patients should arrive 15 minutes before an in-person appointment for registration and verification.

For telehealth appointments, patients should join the video consultation 5 minutes before the scheduled time.

Appointment cancellation or rescheduling should be done at least 24 hours in advance.

Emergency cases should not wait for regular appointments and should contact emergency services immediately.
