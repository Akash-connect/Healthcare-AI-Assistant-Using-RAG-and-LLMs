Patient Discharge Instructions

After discharge, patients should follow the instructions given by the healthcare provider.

Patients should take prescribed medicines as directed and should not stop medication without consulting the provider.

Patients should attend follow-up appointments as scheduled.

Patients should keep the wound area clean and dry if wound care instructions are provided.

Patients should contact the healthcare provider if symptoms worsen, fever develops, pain increases, or unexpected side effects occur.

Patients should seek emergency care for severe chest pain, difficulty breathing, sudden weakness, severe bleeding, or loss of consciousness.
