Medication Refill Policy

Patients can request medication refills through the patient portal, phone call, or telehealth consultation.

Refill requests are allowed only for medicines already prescribed by the healthcare provider.

The refill request should include patient name, medication name, dosage, pharmacy details, and last prescription date.

Refills may take 24 to 48 business hours for review and approval.

Some medicines may require laboratory reports, follow-up consultation, or in-person examination before refill approval.

Controlled substances and high-risk medicines cannot be refilled automatically.

The assistant must not recommend new medicines or change dosage. Patients should consult a licensed healthcare provider for medication changes.
