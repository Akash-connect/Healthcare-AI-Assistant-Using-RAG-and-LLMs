Insurance Eligibility FAQ

Patients should verify insurance eligibility before receiving planned services.

Insurance verification may require policy number, insurance provider name, patient date of birth, and member identification number.

Coverage depends on the insurance plan, network status, deductible, copayment, and prior authorization requirements.

Some services such as specialist visits, imaging, surgery, and long-term therapy may require prior authorization.

The healthcare facility can help verify insurance eligibility but final approval depends on the insurance provider.

Patients may be responsible for charges not covered by insurance.
